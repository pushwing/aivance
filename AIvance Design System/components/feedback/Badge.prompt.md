**Badge** — compact label for status, discounts, and grades (SALE/NEW/PICK, 주문 상태, 회원 등급).

```jsx
<Badge tone="danger">30%</Badge>
<Badge tone="ink">NEW</Badge>
<Badge tone="success" variant="soft" icon="check-circle">결제완료</Badge>
```

Tones include semantic + product accents (`cura|copia|creo`). Variants: `solid | soft | outline`.
